A little bit about me...

I am a software engineer by profession, and passion. I also enjoy photography, travel and music.

I work at Google within the Cloud and Developer Infrastructure group that builds the platform and tools used within Google to power its services, as well as developers using Google's cloud platform.

Previously, I was a Software Architect at Microsoft working on Azure, .NET and the web stack (ASP.NET and more), as well as Bing mobile over the course of 16+ years.

I am also the creator of Script#, an OSS c# to javascript compiler. I share these personal projects via GitHub.
